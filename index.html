Common Themes

These projects look different on the surface, but they share a set of recurring principles:

Structured Data

Every project starts with real data — certification catalogs, job classifications, usage statistics, story archetypes — and builds structure around it.

Visible Systems

Nothing is hidden behind a black box. Pipelines, processes, and logic are exposed so visitors can see how things work, not just what they produce.

Layered Pipelines

Data flows through clear stages — extraction, processing, transformation, presentation — each designed to be understandable on its own.

Exploratory Interfaces

These are tools for exploration, not just display. Visitors interact, filter, and navigate rather than passively reading.

Clarity Over Complexity

Complexity isn't hidden — it's made understandable. The goal is always to make the hard parts accessible without dumbing them down.
